About

Meet The Owner

My name is Miranda Reed, the owner of Savannah’s Sweets! Thank you for visiting my page, I am happy you are here! I started Savannah’s Sweets in September of 2019 as a way to share my hobby with my community. The summer of 2019 brought many challenges for all of us, Savannah’s Sweets was the way of overcoming mine. June 2019, my fiance joined the Airforce and left for basic training. Looking for a hobby to fill my time, baking was at the top of my mind. I wanted a way to keep myself busy and bring joy to those around me. Baking was the perfect option and so Savannah’s Sweets was born.

Meet Savannah

Savannah was my beautiful yellow lab. She was always full of energy and happy to greet everyone who came her way. Savannah’s Sweets was created to embody the spirit of Savannah in every way. She passed away in February 2020, and is remembered with every order.
